The free course

Start Here — the Free University Beginner Course

Step by step, in order, free on YouTube. You'll learn to sit & position your hands · read notes, key signatures (to 1 sharp / 1 flat) & time (2/4, 3/4, 4/4) · chords · pedal · play with both hands · and perform your first real pieces.

Lesson 1 — Warm Up the Keyboard

Your very first lesson: how to sit, find your hand shape, and get comfortable at the keyboard.

Then continue in order — 41 lessons:

  • C Position (right & left hand)
  • Reading notes & the Grand Staff
  • Intervals (2nds → octaves)
  • Chords: C, G7, F, D7
  • Sharps, flats & naturals
  • Scales: C, G, F
  • Time signatures & rhythm
  • Your first real pieces
