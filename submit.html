Good to know

How to record so I can really hear you

📱 Phone placement

Put the phone a little to the side of the piano, about an arm's length away — not on the piano itself (it buzzes). Quiet room, one take, no editing needed. Mistakes are welcome: they're exactly what feedback is for.

🙌 Hands-only video

Film from slightly above and to the side, so I can see your fingers and wrists on the keys. This lets me check your round hand, curved fingers and fingertip contact — the foundation of everything.

🔒 Your privacy

Recordings are used only to prepare your feedback — never published, never shared. Audio-only is completely fine. You choose how much to show.

🤖 Honest note

Feedback is prepared in my studio with the help of an AI assistant trained on my own lessons and method — and during this pilot I personally review every single feedback before it reaches you. You get my standards, faster.
